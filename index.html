Our Founder

And his most famous riddle

Where do you look for someone who's never really there
Always on a staircase but never on a stair

The founder of this unusual school is an equally strange man, Albert Ellingham. A rich twentieth-century businessman, he envisioned a place where teaching is far from rigid academics, tedious hours, and strict discipline. He wanted to make a wonderful place full of riddles, twisting pathways, and gardens. “A place,” he said, “where learning is a game.”
